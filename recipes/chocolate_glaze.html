Chocolate Glaze

Who doesn't like a chocolate donut!?

In this recipe we will explain how you can make a classic chocolate glazed donut from scratch.

You will need just a few ingredients and in less that 5 minutes you are making chocolate glazed donuts!

Ingredients

  • 1k Powdered sugar
  • 125g Water
  • 250g Cocoa powder
  • 15g Butter
  • 25g Milk
  • 2 caps Vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Weigh out all ingredients
  2. Sift the powdered sugar and cocoa powder
  3. Mix together
  4. Add the wet ingredients to the powdered sugar and cocoa and mix until all lumps are gone.
