Maple Glaze

The maple glaze for that maple bacon donut!

In this recipe we will explain how you can make a maple glaze from scratch.

You will need just a few ingredients and in less that 5 minutes you are making maple glazed donuts!

Ingredients

  • 1k Powdered sugar
  • 90g Water
  • 150g prepared maple icing
  • 2 caps Maple extract

Steps

  1. Weigh out all ingredients
  2. Sift the powdered sugar
  3. Add the wet ingredients to the powdered sugar and mix until all lumps are gone.
