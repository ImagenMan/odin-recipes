Honey Glaze

A simple classic

In this recipe we will explain how you can make a classic honey glazed donut from scratch.

You will need just a few ingredients and in less that 5 minutes you are making glazed donuts!

Ingredients

  • Powdered sugar
  • Water
  • vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Weigh out 1k powdered sugar
  2. Add 175g water then 1 cap of vanilla
  3. Mix together until smooth
